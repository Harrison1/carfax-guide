Email marketing involves sending out emails to potential new and repeat customers in order to engage them, generate brand awareness, and increase the chances of a sale.

While email marketing can be an excellent tool for finding new customers, itâ€™s also invaluable for maintaining connections with existing customers.

Email marketing, when done right, can be a very effective tactic for spurring action from your customers and potential buyers.

The key is to use the data available to you to segment your lists according to where shoppers are in their car buying process. and then send them an email that is relevant to their goals.

For example, if someone had completed a form on your website expressing interest in a specific make/model but has not yet bought a car, you could send them an email invite to a special preview of the next model year and entice them to come in to see all the exciting new features in the new model.

OPEN RATE

This metric helps determine how engaged your email audience is.

CLICK-THRU RATE

This metric is a good indication of whether the email itself succeeded in piquing the interest of the consumer.

UNSUBSCRIBE RATE

This metric is crucial, as it determines if people are finding value in the emails being sent to them.
