Videos published online should be relatively short (from one to three minutes) and provide value to viewers.

Videos and other types of rich media (which means anything that needs technology to make it function, like Flash) should all be concise and easy-to-use.

Video content is important for your overall marketing efforts for a few reasons:

Videos are highly effective for communicating a lot of information in a short time, so this medium lends itself well to customer education.

Car buying has a strong visual component. Videos can show the physical aspects of a vehicle more effectively than photos alone, which helps buyers picture themselves in the car.

Videos are important for organic search and often rank well in search engines.

Watch Time:

This metric tells you how much of the video is being viewed.

Research shows you only have 10 seconds to engage an online audience, so this is a good benchmark to determine if your videos are effective.

If your videos aren’t being watched or engaged with, make sure you’re not focusing solely on your dealership but rather a broad topic, such as car maintenance. This will help attract attention to your dealership.
